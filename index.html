How to Play

Glen Cup Bingo turns every golf round into a fun, competitive challenge.
Tap a field whenever the described golf moment happens.
Five marked fields in a row — horizontally, vertically or diagonally — score a Bingo and win the pot.

Field Legend

Each field represents a typical moment on the golf course. Here are a few examples:

  • Long and strong down the middle-Drive — A perfect drive straight down the fairway.
  • GIR Par 3 — Green in regulation on a Par 3.
  • 3-Put — The putt nobody wanted.
  • Slice Deluxe — An unintentional very big curve away from the target.
  • Ice on the ball - tee shot — A tee shot that goes so very high it might as well have ice /snow on it.

(You can expand this list with all 25 fields.)

Tips for a Great Bingo Round

  • Keep an eye on your own shots and your playing partners.
  • Mark only real events — fairness keeps the game fun.
  • Enjoy the round and celebrate the funny moments.
  • Play at a steady pace to keep the game flowing.
  • Agree on pot rules before the round starts.
