📘 How to Play ▼

Turns every buddy golf round into a fun, competitive challenge.

You decide with the buddies what you want to play for: a drink at hole 19, a pot or just the fun – whatever you like.

Decide if you want to play the smaller 4×4 version or the original 5×5 grid.

If you register you can create your own teams and follow the results of the others (feature planned).

You can reset your own grid anytime and start again with a fresh, randomly shuffled grid.

Tap a field whenever the described golf moment happens.
All fields marked in a row – horizontally, vertically or diagonally – score a BINGO and win.
Just shout it out!
