⛳ Golf Bingo

The Fun Golf Bingo turns every golf round into a fun, competitive challenge.

📘 How to Play ▼

Tap a field whenever the described golf moment happens.
Five marked fields in a row — horizontally, vertically or diagonally — score a Bingo and win.

  • 🍺 Drink at the clubhouse
  • 🐦 Bingo mascot (traveling trophy)
  • 🏆 Honor point in your group
  • 🎉 Surprise reward
